My Own Summer (Shove It) by Deftones
This song jumps right into an intense guitar riff. The singers soft-like voice allows it to work with the iconic instrumental. When the chorus comes though, the vocalist is no longer soft. He screams. But it becomes quiet again. It is a cycle. If you have ever wanted to feel like you're falling in love with a sneaky killer, this is a perfect song.
