SONGS

Tears by Boy Harsher
Lets take it back to the 1980's clubs but in 2021! This track, released 2 years ago feels as though you're in an 80s New York City club. The instrumental is very synthy and disco. The vocals are mesmerizing, like a man who is definitely immersed into the club scene. This song has to do with moving on from parts of our lives that no longer serve us.

Melody by PlusTwo
To begin, this song is immediately very otherworldly in its feel. Most certainly a product of its time in the early 1980’s disco scene. The melody of Melody creates an energy that is nothing short of colorful and up-beat, invoking images of the brilliant clubbing scene of Studio 54 or any rave venue akin to it. The first verse describes someone wishing to get away from their struggles, and as the chorus kicks in, the carefree sound of the song serves to positively carry the listener away.

Bicep by TR/ST
This song has a more upbeat instrumental. It invokes a feeling of overcoming some uncomfortable feelings. It is a human thing to do. We crash but we still live. Each passing day, we cope with what we experience--like exercising a muscle, but inside we are ravaged. It is how the world was designed.
