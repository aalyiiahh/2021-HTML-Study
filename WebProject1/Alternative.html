SONGS

Colors by Black Pumas
This song is simply about the beauty of what we think of as common. It starts out sweet, the vocals smooth and the instrumental providing a nostalgic and uplifting mood. The chorus is even more soulful. You can feel the passion and awe of the colors that the artist talks about. As he says "It's a good day for me" and it will be when you hear this song.

Famous Last Words by My Chemical Romance
Prepare to be hit with an intense intro. It will make you want to rock out and scream out. In this song, MCR talks about living in a world that doesn't listen. It's hard when no one hears you, you feel inferior and could even become it. But MCR says otherwise, no matter how hard it is, never be afraid to do it alone. Forgive yourself because you're all you got. Soon enough you'll be heard.

The Past Is A Grotesque Animal by Of Montreal
You will feel like you're on an uphill climb listening to this song. This song has to deal with how we dont realize our perceptions until after they happen. You can think something happened one way until a few years later, you realize what you were missing. This 11 minute song feels like it reaches a climax but it just becomes more disjointed and almost nauseating at how packed and tense it builds up to without a release.
